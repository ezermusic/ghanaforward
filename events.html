Featured Event

Ghana Economic Forum 2025

May 20-21, 2025
9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Kempinski Hotel Gold Coast City, Accra

Join us for the premiere economic development event of the year. The Ghana Economic Forum brings together policymakers, business leaders, academics, and civil society to address key economic challenges and opportunities facing Ghana. This year's theme focuses on "Resilient Growth: Building a Sustainable Economy for All Ghanaians."

Featured Speakers

Dr. Kwame Addo

Former Minister of Finance

Dr. Kwame Addo is a renowned economist with over 20 years of experience in public finance and economic policy. He served as Ghana's Minister of Finance from 2018 to 2022 and currently leads the Economic Policy Think Tank.

Prof. Abena Osei

Director, Ghana Institute of Management

Professor Abena Osei is a leading expert in sustainable development and social policy. Her research focuses on inclusive economic growth and poverty reduction strategies in West Africa.

Mr. Joseph Mensah

CEO, Ghana Infrastructure Investment Fund

With extensive experience in infrastructure development and project finance, Mr. Mensah has been instrumental in attracting over $2 billion in investment to critical infrastructure projects across Ghana.

Ms. Akosua Frimpong

Tech Entrepreneur & Innovator

Akosua Frimpong is the founder of TechGhana, one of Africa's fastest-growing technology startups. Her work focuses on digital solutions for agriculture, education, and financial inclusion.

Dr. Emmanuel Darko

Environmental Policy Specialist

Dr. Darko is an internationally recognized authority on climate-resilient economic development. He advises several West African governments on sustainable resource management and green growth strategies.
