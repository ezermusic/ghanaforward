About Ghana Forward

Building Our Nation Together Through Vision, Leadership, and Collective Action

Our Vision

Ghana Forward envisions a prosperous, innovative, and equitable Ghana that stands as a beacon of development in Africa. We see a nation where every citizen has access to quality education, healthcare, and economic opportunities, empowered to contribute to national growth.

We strive for a Ghana that celebrates its rich cultural heritage while embracing innovation and sustainable development practices, creating a resilient society for generations to come.

Our Mission

Our mission is to catalyze positive transformation in Ghana through evidence-based policy advocacy, civic education, and inclusive dialogue. We bridge the gap between citizens, experts, and policymakers to create sustainable solutions to national challenges.

Through research, public engagement, and strategic partnerships, we aim to foster transparent governance, economic opportunity, and social progress that benefits all Ghanaians.

Our Journey

2018

Foundation

Ghana Forward was established by a group of concerned professionals seeking to create positive change in Ghana's governance and development.

2019

First Policy Summit

Launched our inaugural Policy Summit bringing together experts, officials, and citizens to address economic development challenges.

2020

Digital Civic Education Program

Rolled out our digital civic education initiative reaching over 100,000 Ghanaians with essential information about governance.

2021

Regional Expansion

Opened regional chapters in Kumasi, Tamale, and Takoradi to increase nationwide impact and localize advocacy efforts.

2022

National Policy Influence

Our research and advocacy directly influenced three major national policies on education, healthcare accessibility, and transparency.

2023

African Cooperation Initiative

Launched partnerships with similar organizations across Africa to share best practices and amplify continental impact.

2024

Ghana Forward Institute

Established the Ghana Forward Institute for advanced research and leadership development in public policy and governance.
