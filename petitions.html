Success Stories

See how our collective voices have created real change across Ghana

Kumasi Road Renovation Project

After collecting over 20,000 signatures, our petition led to the Ministry of Roads and Highways approving a comprehensive renovation of the Kumasi-Accra Highway, reducing accidents by 45% and cutting travel time by a third.

The government also implemented new road maintenance standards that will prevent similar deterioration in the future.

Completed: February 2025 23,456 signatories

Clean Water for Volta Region

Our petition with 15,000 signatures successfully lobbied for a new water treatment facility in the Volta Region, providing clean drinking water to over 50,000 residents who previously relied on contaminated sources.

The initiative also included education programs on water conservation and hygiene practices.

Completed: January 2025 15,789 signatories

Digital Classrooms Initiative

With 18,000 signatures, our petition secured funding for digital classrooms in 50 rural schools across Ghana, bringing modern educational technology to over 15,000 students who previously had no access to computers.

The project included teacher training programs and ongoing technical support to ensure sustainability.

Completed: December 2024 18,234 signatories
